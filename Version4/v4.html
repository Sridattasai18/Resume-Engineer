FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

▸How do I get a PDF from the LaTeX code?
Copy the LaTeX code → go to overleaf.com → New Project → Blank → paste → click Compile → Download PDF. Takes under a minute.
▸How does the Upload mode work?
All files (including PDFs) are extracted to plain text client-side using PDF.js before being sent to the selected AI provider. This keeps your data local and secure.
▸Is this only for freshers / CS students?
No — it works for anyone. The section order and templates are optimised for fresher CS resumes by default, but the LaTeX mode works with any existing resume structure regardless of experience level or domain.
